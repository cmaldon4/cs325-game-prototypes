Preserve Earth

  • High Concept: This game is an endurance game to test reflexes while self competing in order to save the Earth's environemnt by watering plants for as long as possible and preventing trash from littering the garden.
  • Theme: The theme used was preservationist.
  • Mandated Variety: The input used keyboard and clicking only, with a combination of deterministc and randomness. The play style is competitor and performer, while the genre is racing/platformer.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player experience intended is for the player to implement astute focus. It should feel similar to survival or the other all out modes offered in other video games.
  • Gameplay: The game starts with the player in the middle of the screen. The player can move left or right, using the corresponding arrow keys on the keyboard. A timer is going which represents the score (high score correlates to longer lasting play time). Over time, an alert will pop up which is a plant requesting water. The character can move to the alert, if when they're touching the alert, the player presses the down key, the plant is watered and the alert is replaced with a heart. The key functionality also works with the w, a, s, d keys with w being up, a being left, s being down, and d being right. Every 10 seconds, the time between the alerts appearing decreases. If an alert is not watered within 4.8 seconds, a life is lost. While this is going on, pieces of trash will also fall from the top of the screen that have to be clicked. If the pieces of trash reach the x axis of which the character is walking on, a life is lost. Once three lives are lost, the game ends, and the screen fades to black leaving the final score and a message. The pieces of trash that emerge will be added one every 10 seconds. Each time a piece of trash is clicked, the x axis of the trash will change, it will move to be above the visible screen, and it will be assigned a random velocity. The game will be without falling trash for the first 10 seconds, and a new piece of trash will be added every 10 seconds until all 7 pieces of trash have become visible.
  • Prototype Goal: The game mechanic being evaluated in this prototype is movement based target acquisition with endurance performance assessment, with a multitasking element.
  • Features: This game is unique in that you have to juggle two very different tasks and it really tests your reflexes and attention skills.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting for this game is our planet needs to be protected by preventing pollution and giving it the care it needs to survive.
  • Story: There is no story other than the premise mentioned above.
  • Target Audience: The target audience is all of those who care for the Earth and who like to test their reflexes and endurance.
  • Play Time: Indefinite.
  • Strategies: No strategies other than to practice.
